Our Story

In these challenging times, it is natural to feel overwhelmed and apprehensive about the future.

Nameit Homeit is dedicated to providing technology support, for over 4 Crore Micro-Businesses in India, to prosper.

We are addressing this Social Cause with the aid of Nameit Homeit Q-Commerce and SaaS E-commerce unified platform transforming the Food value chain.

​Connecting all the stakeholders in the Food value chain, Nameit Homeit’s Digital Transformation platform is reducing food wastage by transitioning from the existing supply-push model to a demand-pull model impacting the way we approach business.

With Nameit Homeit’s assistance, retailers and wholesalers can expand their potential by digitizing and bridging their knowledge gap.

​We recognize the difficulties that come with running a business and are here to offer our unwavering support to help micro businesses thrive.
